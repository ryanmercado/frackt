About

This Project was started by Ryan Mercado, a Computer Engineering student from the University of Texas. Beginning in early October, pre-existing interest in complex fractals stemmed into this idea for a unique type of NFT art composed of fractals. Ryan generated the first hundred fractals and dove into the intricacies of web3, learning everything he would need to make project FRACKT a reality. Months of hard work later and the project is soon to be released for sale. The team hopes that people recognize and appreciate the FRACKT unique value.
