When will the public sale be live?

The presale will be on January 14th at 4pm CST, but they will not be revealed until 24 hours later.

How many FRACKTs will there be?

250 will be available to mint on January 14th, but eventually there will be a total of 1000.

What will it cost to buy a FRACKT?

FRACKTs will start at 0.06 ETH

How is this project different from others?

Most NFT projects use a couple dozen layers to generate thousands of images that all look very similar. FRACKT uses math to create complex fractals that are completely different from each other, with some varying color.

Will some FRACKTs be rarer than others?

Since they were not generated with layers, no FRACKT is rarer than another, although you may think some look cooler.

What is the goal for the community?

The hope is that the beauty of complex fractals will bring together a community of fractal appreciation, and hopefully one day this community will be a part of the metaverse.

Who is the team behind this?

The team is small, and led by Ryan Mercado, a Computer Engineer at the University of Texas that was inspired by his love for complex math. Hundreds of hours have been poured into this project in the last months to get to this point.

Is there a minting limit for FRACKT?

You can mint up to 15 FRACKTs at a time

Where can I buy and sell my FRACKT?

Minting will happen on this website, frackt.io, but resale will be available on opensea. And the team will take 5% of market sales to put towards future projects.

What are the specs?

FRACKTs will be stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain, hosted decentralized on IPFS. Each FRACKT will be 3840x3840 pixels in the non-compressed .bmp format
